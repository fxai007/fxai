Help Center & FAQs

How does FXAI work? ▼

FXAI analyzes multiple timeframe charts to provide AI-powered trade setups with a high probability of success.

What is the minimum risk-reward ratio? ▼

FXAI ensures a minimum risk-reward ratio of **1:2 or higher** for every trade recommendation.

Is FXAI beginner-friendly? ▼

Yes! FXAI is designed for traders of all levels, with easy-to-understand trade setups and AI-powered insights.

Is there a free trial? ▼

Yes! FXAI offers a **7-day free trial** so you can experience our AI trade analysis before subscribing.

What happens after the free trial? ▼

After 7 days, you will be prompted to subscribe for **$25/month** to continue accessing FXAI's features.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period.

Does FXAI guarantee profits? ▼

No. FXAI provides AI-powered trade analysis, but all trading carries risk, and past performance does not guarantee future results.

Does FXAI support stock or crypto trading? ▼

Currently, FXAI is focused on **Forex trading**. Future updates may include stocks and cryptocurrencies.

What happens if my payment fails? ▼

If your payment fails, your subscription will be paused, and you will receive a notification to update your payment details.

Is my data secure with FXAI? ▼

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. FXAI does not share personal information with third parties.
